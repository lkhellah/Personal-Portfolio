Offline LLM Tutor for Math Education
(Proof of Concept)

As part of a final project for a Large Language Models course, I explored how offline AI tools can support education in underserved areas. I evaluated Qwen2.5-Math on elementary-level math questions using a Colab-based demo.

The model produced step-by-step solutions for problems aimed at young learners, like: "Lily has 18 apples. She gives 7 to a friend..."

While the system is not yet interactive or deployable, it demonstrates how language models can be adapted for low-resource educational settings.
